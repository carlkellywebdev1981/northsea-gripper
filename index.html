ABOUT

The North Sea Gripper was developed to provide the protection and dexterity required by the modern offshore diver. It enables him to perform any task or operate any tool with accuracy and confidence! The glove is robust. Manufactured to EN standards it provides a high level of protection against cuts, abrasion and chemical contact without limiting the divers tactile ability. The gauntlet is of a supple neoprene that mates intimately with the hot water suit preventing rolling down,or flapping even while working in the mud, It also prevents ingress of mud and egress of hot water. The North Sea Gripper has been trialled extensively over the last few years form Asia to the North sea. Not one pair has been returned and was hot carded on the Seven Atlantic as the glove the divers would like to see as standard issue!

FEATURES

  • Originally designed for deep/commerical diving
  • Ultra hard wearing
  • Cut, tear, puncture and abrasion resistant
  • Penetration/Permeation Resistant to Chemicals
  • Protective 5mm compressed Neoprene gaunlet
